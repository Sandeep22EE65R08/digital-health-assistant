About the project

AI Digital Health Assistant

Simplifying Healthcare Information with AI

AI Digital Health Assistant is an intelligent web application designed to help users understand medical symptoms, laboratory reports, and healthcare information in simple Hindi and English.

Powered by Google Gemini AI, the platform analyzes symptoms, interprets medical reports, identifies potential health concerns, and provides patient-friendly explanations that are easy to understand. Users can upload PDFs, scanned documents, or report images and receive clear insights without struggling with complex medical terminology.

Key Features

AI-powered symptom analysis

Describe how you feel in plain language and get a structured, AI-driven assessment of possible causes and next steps.

Medical report interpretation

Every parameter in your lab report is mapped to its normal range with a plain-English explanation of what it means.

Doctor & specialist recommendations

Suggests the right type of specialist (cardiologist, endocrinologist, etc.) based on the pattern of your symptoms or results.

Emergency risk detection

Flags red-flag symptoms (chest pain, stroke signs, breathing trouble) and directs you to call emergency services immediately.

Personalized health insights

Answers adapt to your specific symptoms, age cues and report values — not generic advice copied from the internet.

Hindi & English language support

Switch the response language on every page — English, Hindi or Hinglish — so the explanation feels natural to you.

Patient-friendly explanations

No jargon walls. Complex medical terms are translated into short, calm sentences anyone can follow.

PDF and image report processing

Upload a PDF, a scan, or a phone photo — including handwritten ranges. The vision model reads them directly, no retyping.

Benefits
