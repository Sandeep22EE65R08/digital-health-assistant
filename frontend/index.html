How It Works

Frequently asked questions

Is this a real doctor or medical advice?

No. This is an AI-powered explainer for educational use only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for diagnosis, treatment, or anything urgent.

Is my data stored or shared?

Your inputs are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate a response and are not stored on our servers. We don't have user accounts, analytics tracking, or ads.

Which file formats can I upload for reports?

PDF (both text-based and scanned), plus photos in JPG, PNG and WebP up to 10 MB. Scanned PDFs and photos are read by AI vision — handwriting and printed values both work.

Which languages are supported?

English, Hindi, and Hinglish. Pick your language on each page and the assistant will reply in that language.

Can the AI make mistakes?

Yes. AI models can misinterpret values, miss context or hallucinate. Treat every answer as a starting point for a conversation with a qualified clinician, never as a final word.

Is it really free?

Yes — fully free, no signup, no paywalls. The source code is open on GitHub.

What should I do in an emergency?

Don't use this app. Call your local emergency number immediately (India: 112, UK: 999, US: 911). Red-flag symptoms include chest pain, trouble breathing, signs of stroke, severe bleeding, or sudden severe headache.
