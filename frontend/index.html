How it works

Three simple steps — no signup, no ads, no data stored.

  1. 1

    Tell us what's happening

    Describe your symptoms in plain language (English, Hindi or Hinglish) or upload a lab report PDF or photo. You can add age, gender and known conditions for better context.

  2. 2

    AI reads & explains

    Google Gemini reads your input — including scanned reports via AI vision — and writes a calm, structured explanation: possible causes, severity, suggested doctor, tests and home care.

  3. 3

    Ask follow-up questions

    Keep the conversation going. The assistant remembers what you've shared, so each answer builds on the last — just like talking to a knowledgeable friend.

About this project

An open-source, educational health companion built to make medical information easier to understand.

Why it exists

Most people get a lab report or feel a symptom and end up doom-scrolling unclear search results. This assistant gives a calm, structured first read — so you walk into the doctor's office with better questions, not more fear.

What it is — and isn't

It's an AI explainer for symptoms and reports. It is not a diagnosis, prescription, or substitute for a qualified clinician. For anything urgent (chest pain, breathing trouble, severe bleeding, stroke signs), call your local emergency number immediately.

Open & free

The full source code lives on GitHub. Anyone can read it, audit it, or self-host it. Powered by Google Gemini.

Frequently asked questions

Is this a real doctor or medical advice?

No. This is an AI-powered explainer for educational use only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for diagnosis, treatment, or anything urgent.

Is my data stored or shared?

Your inputs are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate a response and are not stored on our servers. We don't have user accounts, analytics tracking, or ads.

Which file formats can I upload for reports?

PDF (both text-based and scanned), plus photos in JPG, PNG and WebP up to 10 MB. Scanned PDFs and photos are read by AI vision — handwriting and printed values both work.

Which languages are supported?

English, Hindi, and Hinglish. Pick your language on each page and the assistant will reply in that language.

Is it really free?

Yes — fully free, no signup, no paywalls. The source code is open on GitHub.

What should I do in an emergency?

Don't use this app. Call your local emergency number immediately (India: 112, UK: 999, US: 911). Red-flag symptoms include chest pain, trouble breathing, signs of stroke, severe bleeding, or sudden severe headache.

Privacy & safety

Plain-English summary of how this app handles your information.

  • No accounts. There's no signup, login, or user profile. Nothing personal is required to use the app.
  • No storage of your inputs. Symptoms, reports and chat messages are forwarded to the AI provider for that one request and are not saved to any database on our side.
  • AI provider. Requests go to Google's Gemini API. Their data-handling terms apply to in-transit processing. Don't paste personal identifiers (full name, ID numbers) into the chat.
  • No ads, no trackers. The site doesn't run advertising scripts or analytics that profile you.
  • Educational use only. Output is informational. It is not a medical diagnosis and must not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis or treatment.
  • Emergencies. If you suspect a medical emergency, call your local emergency number immediately.
